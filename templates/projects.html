ISRO Projects

Chandrayaan

India's lunar exploration program aimed at studying the Moon's surface, mineral composition, and water presence. Chandrayaan missions represent one of ISRO’s most successful planetary exploration programs.

Mangalyaan

The Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) successfully placed India’s first spacecraft into Mars orbit in 2014. It made India the first nation to reach Mars orbit on its first attempt.

Gaganyaan

Gaganyaan is India's ambitious human spaceflight mission designed to send Indian astronauts into space using an Indian launch vehicle and spacecraft developed by ISRO.
